Top 5 Trading Journals Online

1. TraderSync

TraderSync is a powerful trading journal platform designed to help traders analyze performance, identify mistakes, and improve strategy. It supports multiple asset classes including stocks, options, futures, and crypto.

Key Features

  • Trade Importing: Seamless integration with brokers for automatic trade uploads.
  • AI-Powered Insights: Detects patterns and behavioral tendencies to optimize decision-making.
  • Market Replay: Revisit trades with historical chart playback to evaluate timing and execution.
  • Tagging System: Categorize trades by strategy, setup, or emotion for deeper analysis.
  • Performance Metrics: Track win rate, risk/reward, expectancy, and more with visual dashboards.
  • Mobile Access: Journal and review trades on the go with a responsive mobile interface.
  • Multi-Asset Support: Log trades across stocks, options, futures, forex, and crypto.

Why Use TraderSync?

Whether you're a beginner or seasoned trader, TraderSync helps you stay disciplined, learn from past trades, and refine your strategy. Its intuitive design and smart analytics make it a top choice for serious traders.

2. TradesViz

  • Multi-Account Support: Import trades from multiple brokers and platforms into a unified dashboard.
  • Advanced Charting: Visualize trades with customizable charts, overlays, and performance metrics.
  • Strategy Tagging: Categorize trades by strategy, setup, or market condition for granular analysis.
  • Custom Dashboards: Build personalized layouts with widgets to track KPIs and trading goals.
  • Trade Replay: Review trades with historical price action and execution timing.
  • Asset Coverage: Supports stocks, options, futures, forex, and crypto.

Why Use TradesViz?

TradesViz is ideal for scalpers, swing traders, and multi-strategy users who want detailed insights and flexible reporting. Its rich visual tools and data-driven approach make it a top choice for serious traders.

3. EdgeWonk

EdgeWonk is a professional-grade trading journal designed to help traders improve performance through psychological insights, strategy refinement, and data-driven analysis. It supports multiple asset classes including forex, stocks, futures, crypto, and CFDs.

Key Features

  • Trade Simulator: Test and refine strategies using historical data and simulated trades.
  • Emotional Tagging: Track psychological states during trades to identify behavioral patterns.
  • Custom Metrics: Define and monitor personalized performance indicators.
  • Strategy Optimization: Analyze trade setups to improve entry/exit timing and risk management.
  • Journal Entries: Add detailed notes and reflections to each trade for deeper learning.
  • Asset Coverage: Supports forex, stocks, futures, crypto, and CFDs.
  • Performance Dashboard: Visualize key metrics like expectancy, drawdown, and win rate.

Why Use EdgeWonk?

EdgeWonk is ideal for traders who want to go beyond numbers and understand the psychology behind their decisions. Its structured journaling and analytical tools make it a powerful ally for long-term growth and discipline.

4. Tradervue

Tradervue is a powerful trading journal platform designed for traders who want detailed performance analysis and visual trade breakdowns. It supports stocks, futures, options, and forex, making it versatile for various trading styles.

Key Features

  • Trade Importing: Connects with multiple brokers for seamless trade uploads and journaling.
  • Chart Overlays: Automatically generates entry and exit charts for each trade.
  • Performance Reports: Tracks metrics like win rate, average gain/loss, and risk-adjusted returns.
  • Tagging System: Organize trades by strategy, setup, or market condition for deeper insights.
  • Sharing & Collaboration: Share trades with mentors or communities for feedback and learning.
  • Multi-Asset Support: Works with stocks, options, futures, and forex.
  • Trade Notes: Add detailed reflections and observations to each trade for future review.

Why Use Tradervue?

Tradervue is ideal for traders who want to improve through data-driven analysis and visual feedback. Its clean interface and robust reporting tools make it a favorite among professionals and active retail traders alike.

5. Trademetria

Trademetria is a user-friendly trading journal platform designed to help traders track performance, analyze strategies, and improve decision-making. It supports multiple asset classes and offers a balance of simplicity and depth.

Key Features

  • Trade Importing: Connects with various brokers for easy trade uploads and journaling.
  • Customizable Dashboards: Build personalized views with widgets to monitor key metrics and goals.
  • Performance Reports: Analyze win rate, average return, drawdown, and other vital statistics.
  • Strategy Tagging: Organize trades by strategy, setup, or market condition for targeted analysis.
  • Multi-Asset Support: Works with stocks, options, futures, forex, and crypto.
  • Trade Notes: Add reflections and observations to each trade for deeper learning.
  • Affordable Plans: Offers competitive pricing for traders at all levels.

Why Use Trademetria?

Trademetria is ideal for traders who want a straightforward yet powerful journal. Its clean interface, flexible analytics, and multi-asset coverage make it a great choice for both beginners and experienced traders.
